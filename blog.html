10 past 01:00, can't sleep and thinking about food, obviously.

I had the following idea for a recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 4 sirloin steaks
  • butter, for frying
  • 360g linguine
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • concentrated beef stock
  • red wine sauce (see here )

Method:

  1. 24 hours before cooking, salt the steaks and leave in the fridge.
  2. Cook the linuine following pack instructions in salted water, cooking for one minute less than the packet says.
  3. Meanwhile, melt the butter over a medium-high heat and fry the onions with a few drops of the beef stock until golden-brown. Then add the steaks and cook for 2 minutes each side (medium-rare)
  4. Remove the steaks from the pan and leave to rest. Add the linguine straight from the cooking water to the steak pan and stir around to coat in the juices -add more butter if needed.
  5. Plate the steaks, then add a 'nest' of the linguine next to each and add the red wine sauce so that it forms a pool around the two.
